A new coffee brand requiring a sleek visual identity to be applied across a range of products, packaging, online & offline marketing. Vim & Vigour wanted a flat vector illustrated approach to help pad out their identity & add recognisable elements to be re-used across the product family & online / offline marketing content. We undertook a full re-brand with associated elements, created a brand book & applied it across the products, packaging & a sleek new website.
